01 Who I am

I'm Yash Bhirud, a Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science) undergrad at Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology. I like projects that force me to touch the whole stack — designing the schema, wiring the AI model in, and making the UI feel like it was worth the effort.

Most recently I built FindIt, a campus lost-and-found platform that uses TensorFlow.js and OpenCV.js to verify ownership through real photo matching — and MedEase, a hospital management system covering patient records, billing, and role-based dashboards.

02 How I work

Comfortable moving between frontend polish and backend plumbing — wiring a TensorFlow model one hour, tuning CSS grid alignment the next. I default to shipping a working version first, then iterating.
